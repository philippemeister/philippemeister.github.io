Customer Experience Program

The work is summarized below. More detail is available upon request.

Introduction

IMT Insurance wanted to develop a customer experience (CX) program.

Approach

The fist step was to draft 3-year plan for the CX program. The plan describes the next three years of the program in terms of vision, purpose, priciples, strategies, phases of growth, goals, and objectives.

Deliverables

We are currently enacting the first phase of this program, the development phase. In this phase, we drafted the plan, assembled a team, and started working to accomplish the goals for this phase set out in the plan. Regular status updates are provided to the senior leadership at the company, and their responses have been positive.
