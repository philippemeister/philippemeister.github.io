Customer Journey Mapping for a Commercial Insurance Application Process

A brief summary of the work is provided below. More detail is available upon request.

Introduction

The objective was to develop knowledge about how commercial insurance agents experience the process of submitting new business. The UX team worked with the Commercial Lines area to conduct customer journey mapping (CJM) of participants’ experiences from the time they send PDF applications for new business to the time the business is issued. Customer journey mapping is a technique that allows a company to develop knowledge about customers’ experiences as they move through a process.

Approach

The approach included internal and external interviews to develop a customer journey map. First, UX interviewed four commercial lines underwriters and developed an assumption customer journey map. The assumption-based map gathered our internal understanding of the process. Second, UX interviewed 12 external participants and developed a research-based customer journey map. The research-based map verified that participants generally think highly of the IMT application process and underwriters, and it described aspects of the process including steps, pains, needs, positives, and opportunities for improvement.

Deliverables

The main deliverables were a report of the reserach and a reserach-based customer jounrey map. The report described the research process and results. The customer journey map visualized the steps in the process, needs, positive aspects, and opportunites for improvement.

Results and Recommendations

The customer journey map suggests that participants are generally complimentary of the application process and the underwriters. Participants responses suggested potential improvements to the process, and they are summarized below.

  • Increase participants’ opportunities to quote online
  • Improve consistency of underwriter responsiveness and back-up procedures
  • Improve the data transfer from the Agency Management Systems to IMT
  • Provide quick quotes
  • Provide comprehensive proposals (multiline), in clear language, with line-specific details
  • Provide quick access to policy numbers and ID cards
  • Provide ability to bind all policies with one action
  • Provide ability to build, issue, bind, and pay for umbrella policies with everything else
  • Provide option to bill the customer for the down payment
  • Improve awareness about applications that have already been submitted
