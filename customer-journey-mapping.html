Customer Journey Mapping for a Commercial Insurance Application Process

This page summarizes the work. The work can be presented in more detail upon request.

Summary

We conducted customer journey mapping of participants' experiences of submitting new business from the time they submitted applicaitons to the time the business was issued. First, we interviewed four comercial lines underwriters and developed an internal assumption customer journey map. The internal assumption map represented our internal understaing of hte process. Second, we interviewer 12 external particiapnts to develop a reserach-based customer jorurney map. The resrach-based map verified some internal knoweldge and helped us develop new knoweldge of our customers' experiences with the applicaiton process. The reserach-based map described the steps in the process, needs, positive aspects, and opportunites for improvement. Finally, the potential improvements were listed and provided to the commercial lines leadership to inform their decision making.
