Executive Summary

Global Veterinary Link (GVL) is a animal heath software company. We created a deisgn system by document existing practices and developing new pracitices. This work didn't begin with me and it will not stop with me, but I completed some tangible steps to advance the GVL design system. In my time at GVL, I also assessed APIs for international addresses and prototyped designs that were implimented in the system.
