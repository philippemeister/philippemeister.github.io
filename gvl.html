GVL Design System

Summary

Global Veterinary Link (GVL) is an animal heath software company that wanted to improve users' experinces with their products. I created the Global Veterinary Link (GVL) design system to improve product design and development. The process included auditing the product, building working relatonships accross the company, designing new components, and helping impliment a web-based system (Adobe XD, Zeroheight, and Storybook). The new GVL design system supports the design and development of products that provide high-quality user experiences.

Introduction

GVL provides cloud-based animal health products. Their products help veterinarials, animal owners, laboratories, and feed mills save time, reduce paperwork, and improve communication. A newly formed User Experience (UX) team wanted to accomplish two goals: (1) improve the user experince of GVL products, and (2) systematize the design, development, and testing of web components.

Approach

A design system is a set of shared practices and artifacts that support the design and development of digital products such as apps or websites. It may include pattern libraries, style guides, web components, and branding. The UX team and I developed the GVL Design System to improve the user experince of GVL products and systematize the creation of new web components.

The multi-step process was carried our over the course of 3 months:

  1. Identify key stakeholders garnered support for the project among them.
  2. Partner with people in relevanat areas of design, development, product, quality assurance, and marketing.
  3. Assess whether a design sytsem could be useful.
  4. Gather requirements for the design system.
  5. Audit the existing products to identify strengths and weaknesses in the current practices.
  6. Develop design artifacts (integrate materials from design and marketing).
  7. Create a web-based design system to faciliate work between design, development and QA.
  8. Engage partners to grow the GVL design system artifacts and practices.
