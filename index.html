Philippe Meister

Portfolio of work in User Experience Research, Design, and Evaluation

My professional experience is researching, designing, and evaluating human-computer interactions (i.e., user experiences). I have a Ph.D. in human-computer interaction and five years of research and design experience. Previous work includes research for augmented reality aviation weather training and design for web services in the insurance industry. I am also a certified DHS Trusted Tester who can conduct Section 508 Accessibility testing. The selected portfolio projects provide examples my work in the research, design, and evaluation of user experiences. Please view my LinkedIn profile for a list of my work experiences and my Google Scholar profile for a list of my research publications.
