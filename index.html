Introduction

I am a co-major PhD student in Rhetoric and Professional Communication + Human Computer Interaction at Iowa State Univeristy of Science and Technology.

I study the interaction between university information and communication technology systems (ICTs) and university multimodal composition programs with the aim of identifying methods for teaching and learning ICTs in these programs.

I have held teaching, research, and administrative assistantships at ISU. I have taught multimodal communication in the ISUComm program, researched authorship and agency in graduate syllabi in the Department of Political Science, and assisted the administration of a university-wide transition from Blackboard lms to Canvas lms in the Center for Excellence in Learning and Teaching (CELT).
