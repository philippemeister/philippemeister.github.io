Philippe Meister

Portfolio of work in User Experience Research, Design, and Evaluation

I am an evidence-based User Experience (UX) professional who applies human-centered design methods to enhance human well-being and system performance. I have a Ph.D. in Human-Computer Interaction and four years of professional experience working in complex, regulated industries. Of the four years, two focused on conducting human factors research about using augmented reality for aviation weather training, and two focused on conducting generalist UX practice in the insurance industry. Through my training and experience, I have developed the knowledge, skills, and abilities to create experiences that are fit to people. The selected portfolio projects provide examples my work in the research, design, and evaluation of user experiences. For a list of my work experiences, please view my LinkedIn Profile.
