Introduction

At Iowa State University of Science and Technology, I am a co-major PhD student in Rhetoric and Professional Communication + Human Computer Interaction as well as a graduate administrative assistant at the ISU Center for Excellence in Learning and Teaching.

I study the interaction between university information and communication technology systems (ICTs) and university multimodal composition programs with the aim of incorporating ICTs into multimodal composition programs and identifying methods for teaching and learning ICTs in these programs.
