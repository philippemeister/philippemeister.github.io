Platform account and policy user interfaces

This page summarizes the work. The work can be presented in more detail upon request.

Summary

The objective of this work was to design and evaluate account and policy user interfaces for the IMT Platform. The design work was conducted through collaborative design sessions and external interviews. First, the internal working group conducted collaborative design sessions to generate interfaces. Second, ten participants were interviewed to learn about their expereinces with the current system and identify areas for improvement. We used this inrormation to revise our interface, and this was the completion of our first design iteration. The evaluation was conducted through a user evaluation with six particiants. The participants mix of agennts, owners, and cusotmer service representatives. The objective of the formative user evaluation was to identify issues with the interface and define any revisions. We conducted the evaluations and developed a list of interface revisions.
