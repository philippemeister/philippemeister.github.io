Platform account and policy user interfaces

This page summarizes the work. The work can be presented in more detail upon request.

Summary

The objective of this work was to design and evaluate user interfaces for accounts and policies. We were developoing a new platform for the business, and wanted to improve the user experience when servicing accounts and plolicies.

The methods used to generate the interface were collaborative design sessions and external interviews. First, we worked with an internal working group to generate interfaces that would work for them. Second, we interviewed ten participants, a mix of agents, owners, and customer service represetnatives, to learn about their expereinces with the current system and identify areas for improvement. We used this inrormation to revise our interface, and this was the completion of our first design iteration

The methods used to evaluate the interfaces were a formative user evaluation with six particiants, a mix of agennts, owners, and cusotmer service representatives. The objective of the formative user evaluation was to identify issues with the interface and define any revisions. We conducted the evaluations and developed a list of interface revisions.
