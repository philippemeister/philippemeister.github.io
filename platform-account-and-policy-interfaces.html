Platform account and policy user interfaces

The work is summarized below. More detail is available upon request.

Introduction

The objective of this work was to create new account and policy user interfaces for underwriters and agents in the IMT Platform. The IMT Platform is a new internal system that is being developed to create consistency in the servicing of policies accross all lines of busienss.

Approach

A four-step design process was conducted to meet the needs of underwriters and agents. The first step in the process was to conduct collaborative design sessions with the internal working group to generate the interface requirements and mockups. The second step was to conduct interviews with ten participants (agents, customer service representatives, and agency owners) to learn about their expereinces with the current system and identify areas for improvement. This information was used to revise the requirements and low-fidelity mockups. The third step was to create high-fidelity mockups that met the functional requirements and provided approrpriate interactions and visual styling. The fourth step was to coduct a user evaluation with six particiapnts to identify functionality and usability issues with the interface.

Evaluation

A user evaluation was conducted to identify functionality and usability issues with the interface. The six participants included agents, owners, and customer service representatives. The issues were cataloged and rated for severity, and then used to develop a list of interface revisions.
